What Is a Paddleboard Yacht Tour Marbella?

A paddleboard yacht tour Marbella is a private boat charter that includes SUP equipment. You paddle in sheltered coves, then relax on deck. Our fleet of 18 boats ranges from the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) to the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax). Each charter is fully crewed, so you focus on fun.

Why Choose a SUP Charter in Marbella?

Marbella’s coastline offers calm bays ideal for paddleboarding. Cala del Faro, near the Golden Mile, has flat water and rocky cliffs. Cala Cortés, south of Estepona, is a hidden gem. A SUP charter lets you access these spots without crowds. Plus, you control the schedule — stop when you see a quiet cove.

Best Spots for SUP on a Yacht Tour

  • Cala del Faro – Sheltered cove near Marbella with calm water.
  • Cala Cortés – Quiet bay south of Estepona, perfect for beginners.
  • Cabopino – Sandy beach with dunes and clear water.
  • Río Verde – River mouth near San Pedro Alcántara.
  • Sotogrande – Exclusive marina with calm inner harbour.

Your skipper will choose the best location based on wind and swell.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a paddleboard yacht tour Marbella?

A paddleboard yacht tour Marbella is a private boat charter that includes stand-up paddleboard (SUP) equipment. You can paddle in calm coves like Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés, then relax on your yacht. All our charters depart from Puerto Banús and include skipper, fuel, drinks, and snacks.

Which boats offer paddleboard equipment?

Most of our boats can accommodate SUP equipment on request, including the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax), Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax), and Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax). The size of the SUP board depends on boat capacity. Contact us via WhatsApp to confirm availability for your chosen yacht.

How much does a paddleboard yacht tour cost?

Prices start from €749 for 2 hours on the Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39 (Tier A), including skipper, drinks, snacks, and 21% VAT. The Mangusta 80 (Tier B) costs from €4,719 for 4 hours and includes a jet ski. Other boats are priced on request via WhatsApp.

Where can I paddleboard during the tour?

Popular spots include Cala del Faro (a secluded cove near Marbella), Cala Cortés (near Estepona), and the calm waters off Cabopino. Your skipper will recommend the best location based on sea conditions and your preferences.

Do I need to bring my own SUP board?

No, we provide SUP equipment on request for most charters. Boards are inflatable and easy to store. Just let us know when booking. We recommend bringing a swimsuit, sunscreen, and a towel.

Is paddleboarding suitable for beginners?

Yes, stand-up paddleboarding is beginner-friendly in calm waters. Our skippers will help you find a sheltered spot like Cala del Faro where the sea is flat. Life jackets are available if needed.

Can I combine paddleboarding with other activities?

Absolutely. Many guests combine SUP with swimming, snorkelling, or jet skiing. The Mangusta 80 includes a free jet ski. You can also request a fishing rod or snorkel gear. Just ask when booking.
