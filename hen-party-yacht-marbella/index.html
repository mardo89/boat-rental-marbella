Why choose a hen party yacht in Marbella?

Marbella offers 320 days of sunshine a year, calm waters, and a glamorous backdrop. A private yacht lets you skip crowded beach clubs and enjoy your own floating party. You can anchor off Cala del Faro for a swim, cruise past the Golden Mile, or stop for photos at the Puerto Banús marina. Every charter includes a skipper, so you don't need a licence – just bring your playlist and your party spirit.

Popular itineraries for hen parties

Most charters head west towards Estepona or east to Cabopino. Popular stops include Cala del Faro for swimming, Cala Cortés for a secluded cove, and the Río Verde area for dolphin spotting. Your skipper will tailor the route to your group's wishes.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a hen party yacht cost in Marbella?

Our hen party charters start at €749 for 2 hours on an Astondoa 40 or Azimut 39, all inclusive. For a larger group, the Mangusta 80 (12 pax) costs €4,719 for a minimum of 4 hours, with a jet ski included. Other boats are priced on request via WhatsApp.

What is included in a hen party yacht charter?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No extra charges at the dock.

Which boat is best for a hen party of 10-12 people?

For 10-12 guests we recommend the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax), Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax), or Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax). All depart from Puerto Banús and include drinks and snacks.

Can we bring our own decorations or music?

Yes, you are welcome to bring decorations (balloons, banners) and play your own music via Bluetooth or AUX on board. Just avoid anything that might damage the boat or disturb other charters.

Do we need a skipper? Can we drive ourselves?

A licensed skipper is included on every charter. You cannot drive the boat yourself unless you hold a valid Spanish boating licence. The skipper handles navigation so you can relax.

What time do charters start and how long can we book?

Charters typically start from 10:00, 12:00, 14:00, or 16:00. You can book from 2 hours up to a full day (8 hours). Evening sunset cruises are also popular for hen parties.

What happens if the weather is bad on our day?

We monitor forecasts closely. If conditions are unsafe, we reschedule or offer a full refund. For minor weather, the skipper may adjust the route to sheltered areas like Cabopino or Estepona.

How do I book a hen party yacht in Marbella?

Contact us via WhatsApp or our website enquiry form. We'll confirm availability, send you a quote, and take a deposit to secure your date. Balance is due on the day.
