Popular Routes from Puerto Banús

Frequently asked questions

What is included in a motor yacht rental in Marbella?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No extra charges for these items.

How much does it cost to rent a motor yacht in Marbella?

Our Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39) start at €749 for 2 hours and go up to €2,299 for 8 hours. Tier B boats (Mangusta 80 variants) are €4,719 for a minimum 4 hours and include a free jet ski. Other yachts are priced on request.

Where do the motor yachts depart from in Marbella?

All our motor yachts depart from Puerto Banús, the famous marina in Marbella. You can also arrange pickup at Marbella Marina or other nearby points upon request.

How many people can a motor yacht accommodate?

Capacity varies by boat: from 5 guests (Dubhe, Mariah SX21) up to 12 guests (Mangusta 80, Azimut 58, Canados 86, Ferretti 94, K80). Most mid-range boats hold 9–11 guests.

Can I bring my own food and drinks on the yacht?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and additional drinks. Our standard provision includes water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, and snacks. We also offer catering upgrades – ask via WhatsApp.

What is the best time of year for a motor yacht rental in Marbella?

The season runs from April to October, with peak conditions June–September (sea temp 22–26°C, air 28–35°C). May and October offer quieter marinas and milder temperatures. Year-round rentals possible with weather check.

Do I need a boating license to charter a motor yacht?

No. All our charters include a licensed skipper who handles navigation, docking, and safety. You just relax and enjoy the coast.
