Why choose a yacht for Marbella Pride?

Marbella Pride brings the Costa del Sol to life with parades, parties, and a welcoming atmosphere. A yacht adds exclusivity: you control the guest list, the music, and the route. Cruise past the Golden Mile, drop anchor at Cala del Faro, or watch the sunset over La Concha. With capacities from 5 to 12 guests, there's a boat for every group size.

Sample itinerary for a Pride party

A typical 4-hour charter from Puerto Banús might include:

  • Departure from Puerto Banús marina
  • 30-minute cruise east towards Marbella's Golden Mile
  • Anchor stop at Cala Cortés for swimming and photos
  • Return via Cabopino, with music and drinks throughout
  • Drop-off back at Puerto Banús

Longer charters can reach Estepona or Sotogrande. The skipper will tailor the route to your preferences.

Frequently asked questions

What boats are available for a Marbella Pride yacht party?

Can I bring my own cava on board?

Yes, BYO cava is welcome. We also provide water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, and cava as part of the standard inclusions, plus light snacks.

Is there a DJ option for the party?

Yes, a DJ add-on is available. Please contact us via WhatsApp for pricing and availability. We can arrange a DJ to play your favourite tracks.

What is included in the price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No hidden extras.

Where does the yacht depart from?

All yachts depart from Puerto Banús, Marbella. You can cruise along the coast past the Golden Mile, Cabopino, or Estepona, depending on your chosen route.
