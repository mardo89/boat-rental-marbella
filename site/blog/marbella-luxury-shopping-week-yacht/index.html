Marbella Luxury Shopping Week is the Costa del Sol’s premier retail event, drawing VIP shoppers to the Golden Mile and Puerto Banús for exclusive deals, champagne, and after-hours access. But the real luxury? Arriving by private yacht. Charter a boat from our fleet in Puerto Banús and combine boutique hopping with a Mediterranean cruise — no traffic, no queues, just sea, sun, and style.

Why charter a yacht during Shopping Week?

Marbella’s Golden Mile and Puerto Banús are packed during the event. A private yacht gives you a floating base: dock at the marina, walk to Dior or Cartier, then retreat to your deck for a glass of cava. Our charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, and light snacks — so you can focus on shopping, not logistics. Prices start at €749 for two hours on an Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) or Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax), both departing from Puerto Banús.

Our fleet for VIP shoppers

We operate 18 boats from Puerto Banús, from speedboats to superyachts. For Shopping Week, these are the top picks:

  • Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) – spacious flybridge for sunset drinks after shopping. Price on request.
  • Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) – head-turner with a free jet ski included. Minimum 4h at €4,719.
  • Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax) – ultimate luxury for a group of VIPs. Price on request.
  • Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) – affordable elegance at €749/2h or €2,299/8h.

All charters include skipper, fuel, drinks, snacks, insurance, and 21% VAT. No hidden costs.

How to combine shopping and sailing

Our skippers can tailor the itinerary. A typical day: depart Puerto Banús at 11:00, cruise to Cala del Faro for a swim, dock at Marbella Marina at 14:00 for shopping, then return to Puerto Banús for an evening cocktail. Alternatively, stay moored in Puerto Banús and use the yacht as a private lounge between store visits. We can also arrange a tender to take you directly to the Golden Mile beach clubs.

Pricing that works for groups

Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39) are the most affordable: €749 for 2 hours, €2,299 for 8 hours. Tier B (Mangusta 80) starts at €4,719 for 4 hours and includes a jet ski. For other yachts like the Azimut 58 or Ferretti 94, pricing is on request — contact us via WhatsApp for a custom quote. All prices include VAT, skipper, fuel, and drinks.

Local tips for Shopping Week

Puerto Banús boutiques often extend hours until 22:00 during the event. Book a late-afternoon charter (16:00–20:00) to catch the best light and the evening champagne receptions. After shopping, cruise to Cabopino or Estepona for dinner. La Concha mountain provides a stunning backdrop for photos. Remember to bring sunscreen and a light jacket — the sea breeze can be cool even in June.

Book your VIP yacht experience

Demand is high during Shopping Week. We recommend booking at least a week in advance. Contact us via WhatsApp or the enquiry form on our Boat Rental Marbella page. We’ll confirm availability within 2 hours and can arrange same-day bookings for smaller boats if slots remain. Your yacht awaits.

Frequently asked questions

What is Marbella Luxury Shopping Week?

Marbella Luxury Shopping Week is an annual event (typically June) where designer boutiques along the Golden Mile and Puerto Banús offer exclusive discounts, champagne receptions, and extended hours. It attracts VIP shoppers from across Europe.

How do I combine a yacht charter with Shopping Week?

You can charter a yacht from Puerto Banús, dock for a few hours to shop at nearby stores like Louis Vuitton or Gucci, then continue cruising. Our skippers are flexible with itineraries.

Which yachts are best for a shopping group?

The Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) offers plenty of deck space for a group. For a more intimate trip, the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) is a great option at €749/2h.

Can we dock near the shops?

Yes, Puerto Banús marina has guest berths steps from the main shopping arcade. We can also arrange a tender transfer to Marbella Marina if you prefer.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No hidden fees.

Is the Mangusta 80 available for Shopping Week?

Yes, the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) is available from €4,719 for 4 hours, including a free jet ski. Ideal for making a grand entrance.

How do I book?

Contact us via WhatsApp or the enquiry form on our website. We recommend booking at least a week ahead during Shopping Week.

Can we bring our own shopping bags on board?

Absolutely. There is plenty of storage on all yachts. Our crew will help you stow purchases safely.
