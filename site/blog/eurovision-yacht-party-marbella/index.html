Why a Yacht Party for Eurovision?

The Eurovision Final is a global spectacle, and Marbella's warm May evenings make it the perfect backdrop for a nautical celebration. Instead of a crowded bar or a stuffy living room, you get a private deck, a sea breeze, and the freedom to play your own songbook. Anchor off the coast, watch the sunset over La Concha, and then stream the show on your device. It's a unique blend of glamour and fun that only Marbella can offer.

BYO Songbook: Create Your Own Eurovision Playlist

The beauty of a private yacht is that you control the music. All our boats come with Bluetooth audio systems, so you can connect your phone and play your favourite Eurovision hits. From Waterloo to Euphoria, from Salvador Sobral to Loreen, you decide the soundtrack. You can even host your own voting show with scorecards and flags. The only rule is: no jury rigging.

Sunset Cruise Itinerary

Your Eurovision yacht party typically departs from Puerto Banús. You'll cruise along the Marbella coastline, passing the Golden Mile, Cabopino, and possibly Estepona or Sotogrande depending on your chosen duration. The sunset around 9:00 PM (May) paints the sky in shades of orange and pink, perfect for photos. After sunset, you can anchor near a beach club or return to port to watch the final live. We recommend a 6:00 PM departure to enjoy the full sunset experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we play our own Eurovision playlist on the yacht?

Yes, absolutely. All our yachts have Bluetooth audio systems, so you can connect your phone and play your own Eurovision songbook. We encourage you to bring your favourite tracks, from ABBA to the latest winners, and create your own onboard voting show.

Which boats are best for a Eurovision party?

For groups up to 12, the Azimut 58 Flybridge or Mangusta 80 offer plenty of deck space and a flybridge for dancing. The Ferretti 94 (29m) is ideal for larger parties. If you're a smaller group, the Astondoa 40 (9 pax) or Azimut 39 (11 pax) are great value at €749/2h.

What time does the sunset cruise start?

Sunset in Marbella varies by season, but typically we recommend a 6:00 PM or 7:00 PM departure to catch the golden hour. You can combine the Eurovision Final broadcast (which usually starts around 9:00 PM CEST) with a pre-show sunset sail.

Is the Eurovision Final broadcast available on board?

We don't provide a TV or satellite feed, but you can stream the show on your phone or tablet using our onboard Wi-Fi (subject to signal). Alternatively, you can dock at a beach club or return to port to watch on a big screen.

What's included in the price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). Tier B boats (Mangusta 80) also include a free jet ski. You just bring your songbook and good vibes.

Can we bring our own food and decorations?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own food, decorations, and even flags to celebrate Eurovision. Just let us know in advance so we can ensure the yacht is prepared. We'll provide coolers and serving areas.

How do we book a Eurovision yacht party in Marbella?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp with your preferred date, group size, and boat type. We'll confirm availability and send a booking link. Payment is secure, and we recommend booking at least two weeks in advance for the Eurovision Final.

What's the cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before departure. Within 48 hours, a 50% charge applies. Weather-related cancellations are fully refundable or reschedulable. See our terms for details.
