By Andra Kiirkivi Founder & CEO · Boat Rental Marbella · Updated 20 May 2026 · Reviewed by local Marbella skippers

Starlite Marbella is the summer's highlight on the Costa del Sol, drawing world-class acts to the Nagüeles quarry every July and August. For 2026, combining a yacht charter Starlite Marbella day with a concert evening is the ultimate way to experience the coast. This guide covers a practical day-of itinerary, how the Nagüeles pickup works, and which of our boats fit your group.

Why Combine a Yacht Charter with Starlite Marbella?

The Nagüeles venue sits 10 minutes inland from Puerto Banús, making it easy to spend the day on the water and the evening under the stars. You avoid the midday heat, enjoy the Mediterranean at its best, and arrive at the concert relaxed and refreshed. Our fleet departs from Puerto Banús, so the logistics are simple: boat during the day, taxi to Nagüeles at dusk.

Recommended Day-of Itinerary

Here's a sample schedule for a Starlite concert starting at 21:00 (gates open 20:00):

  • 10:00 – Board your yacht at Puerto Banús. Meet your skipper, stow bags, set off.
  • 10:30–13:00 – Cruise east toward Cabopino or west toward Cala del Faro. Swim stops, sunbathing, drinks.
  • 13:00–14:30 – Lunch at anchor near a beach club (e.g., La Plage Casanis or Trocadero Marbella) or onboard with included snacks and drinks.
  • 14:30–17:00 – More cruising, maybe a jet ski session (included on Mangusta 80 charters).
  • 17:00–18:00 – Return to Puerto Banús, shower (if available at marina facilities), change.
  • 18:30 – Taxi from Puerto Banús to Nagüeles (10 min, ~€15–20).
  • 20:00 – Gates open, enjoy the concert.

Nagüeles Pickup & Transfers

We don't run a shuttle, but we can arrange a pre-booked taxi to meet you at the marina. The drive to Nagüeles is short — about 6 km up the hill. If you're staying on the Golden Mile, we can also coordinate pickup from your hotel or villa before or after the yacht. Just let us know when you book.

Which Boat for Your Group?

Our fleet covers groups from 5 to 12 guests. For smaller parties (up to 9), the Astondoa 40 (€749/2h) or Azimut 39 (€749/2h) are cost-effective options. For larger groups (up to 12), the Mangusta 80 (€4,719/4h) offers more deck space, a flybridge, and a free jet ski. All prices include skipper, fuel, drinks, snacks, and tax — no surprises.

What's Included in Every Charter

Every booking includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). There are no extra charges for the basics. You just bring sunscreen, towels, and your Starlite tickets.

Booking Tips for Starlite 2026

  • Book your yacht early — Starlite weeks are high season, and popular boats like the Azimut 58 Flybridge go fast.
  • Confirm your concert tickets separately — we don't sell them, but we can advise on official resellers.
  • Consider a full-day charter (8h) for maximum flexibility, starting at €2,299 for Tier A boats.
  • If you want a late return after the concert, let us know — we can arrange a skipper to bring you back to the marina.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Puerto Banús from Starlite Marbella?

Puerto Banús is approximately 10 minutes by car from the Nagüeles quarry venue. We can arrange a taxi transfer for you as part of your charter day.

Can we dock the yacht near Starlite?

No, the venue is inland at the Nagüeles quarry, so you cannot dock directly. The standard approach is to enjoy the yacht during the day, then take a short taxi ride to the concert.

What time should we start the yacht charter on a Starlite day?

We recommend a morning departure around 10:00–11:00. This gives you 4–6 hours for cruising, swimming, and lunch before heading to the concert at 20:00.

Do you offer a combined yacht + Starlite ticket package?

We don't sell Starlite tickets directly, but we can include a taxi transfer from Puerto Banús to Nagüeles in your charter booking.

Which boat is best for a Starlite day with 8 guests?

The Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) or Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax) are ideal for small groups. For larger parties, the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) offers more space and a free jet ski.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). No hidden costs.

Can we extend the charter into the evening after Starlite?

Yes, if you'd like to return to the yacht after the concert, we can arrange a late-evening return to Puerto Banús. Just let us know in advance.

Is there parking at Puerto Banús for our car?

Yes, there are several public car parks near the marina. We can advise on the best options when you book.
