By Andra Kiirkivi Founder & CEO · Boat Rental Marbella · Updated 20 May 2026 · Reviewed by local Marbella skippers

The Global Gift Gala Marbella is one of the most anticipated charity events on the Costa del Sol calendar. Each summer, celebrities, philanthropists, and influencers gather along the Golden Mile to raise funds for children's and women's causes. Arriving by yacht from Puerto Banús adds a layer of glamour and convenience, especially when you plan your after-party wisely. Here's how to make the most of your Global Gift Gala Marbella yacht experience, from docking tips to post-gala celebrations.

Why arrive by yacht to the Global Gift Gala?

Arriving by yacht is the ultimate statement at the Global Gift Gala. Puerto Banús is just a short walk or taxi ride from the gala venue, typically located at a luxury hotel like the Marbella Club or Puente Romano. Chartering a boat means you bypass traffic, enjoy pre-gala drinks on deck, and have a private base for the after-party. Our fleet includes boats from 6.4m to 29m, accommodating 5 to 12 guests. For a couple or small group, the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) at €749/2h is a smart choice. Larger groups can opt for the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) from €4,719 for 4 hours, which includes a free jet ski.

  • Tier A (€749/2h – €2,299/8h): Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) and Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax). Perfect for couples or small groups wanting a stylish, cost-effective option.

Yacht arrival logistics

On gala day, plan to arrive at Puerto Banús at least 30 minutes before your charter start time. Our team will meet you at the dock. The marina is secure, and parking is available if you drive. From the marina, the gala venue is a 5-10 minute taxi ride along the Golden Mile. If you prefer to walk, it's a pleasant 20-minute stroll along the beachfront. For a seamless experience, we recommend a 2-hour charter before the gala for pre-drinks and photos, then dock and head to the event. After the gala, return to the boat for the after-party.

After-party ideas on your yacht

The after-party is where your yacht truly shines. Here are three ways to celebrate:

  • Cruise to Cala del Faro: A 20-minute ride from Puerto Banús, this secluded cove offers calm waters for a late-night swim under the stars. Our boats have deck lights and sound systems.
  • Anchor off Cabopino: Head east to Cabopino beach, known for its relaxed vibe. You can moor offshore and enjoy drinks with views of the Marbella skyline.
  • Stay in Puerto Banús: Dock at the marina and walk to clubs like Olivia Valere or Pangea. Your boat serves as a private VIP lounge before or after the club.

All our charters include a cooler with water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, and cava. You can bring your own food or order catering in advance. For larger groups, the Mangusta 80 has ample deck space for dancing.

What to wear for the gala and yacht

The Global Gift Gala dress code is black tie or formal evening wear. For the yacht arrival, elegant cocktail attire works well. Ladies can wear heels but consider bringing flats for the marina walk. Men should opt for a suit or tuxedo. Our skippers are discreet and will assist with docking and boarding. If you're planning a swim during the after-party, bring a change of clothes. The water temperature in summer averages 24°C, so a late-night dip is refreshing.

Booking tips for the Global Gift Gala weekend

Demand for yacht charters spikes during the gala weekend. Book at least two weeks in advance to secure your preferred boat. Tier A boats are popular for smaller groups and often sell out first. For last-minute requests, contact us via WhatsApp – we may have availability on larger vessels. Consider an 8-hour charter (€2,299 for Tier A) to cover pre-gala drinks, the event, and the after-party. If you want to extend into the early hours, we can arrange a late return (subject to marina rules).

Explore Marbella from the water

Beyond the gala, Marbella's coastline offers stunning spots to explore. From Puerto Banús, you can cruise to Estepona (15 NM west) or Sotogrande (25 NM west) for a day trip. The views of La Concha mountain from the sea are unforgettable. Our skippers know the best anchorages, including Cala Cortés and the sandbanks off Río Verde. Frequently asked questions

What is the Global Gift Gala Marbella?

The Global Gift Gala Marbella is a charity event organised by the Global Gift Foundation, raising funds for children's and women's causes. It attracts celebrities, influencers, and philanthropists to Marbella each summer, with a glamorous dinner, auction, and performances at a luxury venue.

Where does the Global Gift Gala take place in Marbella?

The gala is typically held at a luxury hotel or venue along the Golden Mile, such as the Marbella Club Hotel or Puente Romano. The exact location varies each year, but it's always within walking distance of Puerto Banús, making yacht arrivals convenient.

Can I arrive at the gala by yacht?

Yes, arriving by yacht is a popular choice. You can charter a boat from our fleet at Puerto Banús and dock nearby. The gala venue is a short walk or taxi ride from the marina. Our Tier A boats start at €749/2h and include a skipper, drinks, and snacks.

What are the best after-party options after the gala?

After the gala, head to Puerto Banús for clubs like Olivia Valere or Pangea, or enjoy a late dinner at a beach club like Ocean Club Marbella. Another option is to charter a boat for a private after-party cruise along the coast, with drinks and music under the stars.

How do I book a yacht for the Global Gift Gala?

You can book directly through our website or contact us via WhatsApp for availability. Due to high demand during the gala weekend, we recommend booking at least two weeks in advance. Prices start at €749/2h for Tier A boats, with other vessels on request.

What is included in the yacht charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21%). There are no hidden fees. You just bring your party and enjoy.

Can I use the yacht for the after-party?

Absolutely. Many guests extend their charter for the after-party, cruising along the coast to Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés. Our boats are equipped with sound systems and ample deck space. You can also arrange catering or a DJ upon request.

What is the dress code for the Global Gift Gala?

The dress code is black tie or formal evening wear. For the yacht arrival, elegant cocktail attire is fine. Don't forget comfortable shoes for the marina walk. Our skippers are experienced in handling formal guest arrivals.
