Watching the sunset from the water is one of the best experiences you can have on the Costa del Sol. The light turns golden, the sea calms, and the Sierra Blanca mountains glow pink. In this guide, we share the best sunset spots from sea Marbella, where to anchor, and how to book a boat for the perfect evening. All our charters depart from Puerto Banús and include a skipper, drinks, and snacks — starting at €749 for 2 hours.

Why Watch the Sunset from a Boat in Marbella?

From land, the sunset is partly blocked by buildings and hills. From the sea, you get a 360-degree view of the horizon. The light reflects off the water, and you can anchor in a quiet cove away from crowds. The temperature drops pleasantly, and you can enjoy a glass of cava as the sky changes colour. It’s a memory that lasts.

Top Sunset Spots from Sea Marbella

Here are the best locations to anchor for sunset, based on our local knowledge:

  • Cala del Faro – A rocky cove near Cabopino. Calm waters, great for swimming before sunset. 15 minutes from Puerto Banús.
  • Cala Cortés – Small, secluded beach between Marbella and Estepona. Excellent for a quiet evening. 20 minutes from Puerto Banús.
  • Río Verde mouth – Just west of Puerto Banús. Shallow, warm water, and a direct view of the sun setting behind La Concha mountain. 10 minutes from port.
  • Off the Golden Mile – Anchor near the Marbella Club Hotel for a classic view of the Marbella coastline lit by sunset. 15 minutes from Puerto Banús.
  • Puerto Banús entrance – Stay near the marina for a lively atmosphere with other boats. Perfect for a short 2-hour charter. 5 minutes from dock.

The skipper will anchor in a safe location. You can swim, take photos, or simply relax. The return is after sunset, with the lights of Marbella reflecting on the water.

When to Book for the Best Sunset

Sunset times vary by season. In summer (June–August), golden hour runs from 20:00 to 21:30. In spring and autumn, it’s earlier, from 19:00 to 20:30. We recommend a 2-hour charter departing 1 hour before sunset. For a longer experience, book a 4-hour charter and include a stop for dinner at a beach club.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time for sunset from sea Marbella?

The best time is during golden hour, roughly 20:00 to 21:30 in summer (June–August), and earlier in spring and autumn. Aim to depart 1–2 hours before sunset to reach your spot and enjoy the changing light.

Which boat is best for a sunset cruise in Marbella?

For a small group, the Azimut 39 (11 pax) at €749/2h is ideal. For larger parties, the Mangusta 80 (12 pax, €4,719/4h) includes a jet ski. All boats have open decks perfect for watching the sunset.

Can we swim during the sunset charter?

Yes, most sunset charters include a stop for swimming. Popular spots like Cala del Faro and Cala Cortés have calm, clear waters. Your skipper will anchor in a safe area for a dip before the sun goes down.

What is included in the sunset boat rental price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and safety equipment. Spanish IVA (21%) is also included. No hidden costs.

Do we need to bring anything for the sunset cruise?

Just yourself, sunscreen, a towel, and a camera. We provide drinks and snacks. For cooler evenings, bring a light jacket. Sunglasses are useful until the sun dips below the horizon.

How far in advance should we book a sunset charter?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 days ahead, especially in July and August. Last-minute bookings are possible via WhatsApp if availability allows. Weekends fill up quickly.

What happens if the weather is bad for sunset?

Your safety is our priority. If conditions are unsafe, we’ll reschedule or offer a full refund. We monitor forecasts closely and will contact you 24 hours before departure if changes are needed.

Can we customise the sunset route?

Absolutely. Our skippers are flexible. You can choose to stay near Puerto Banús, head toward Cabopino, or explore the coast toward Estepona. Just let us know your preference when booking.
