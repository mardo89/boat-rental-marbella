Why Choose a Sunrise Yoga Yacht in Marbella?

Marbella’s coastline offers calm waters and stunning vistas, making it ideal for early-morning yoga. The quiet hours before 09:00 mean fewer boats and a serene atmosphere. You’ll anchor in sheltered spots like Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés, where the water is mirror-flat. The combination of gentle movement, sea air, and sunrise light boosts your energy for the day ahead. Plus, with our all-inclusive packages, you don’t need to worry about logistics — just show up and flow.

What to Expect on Your Sunrise Yoga Charter

Your experience begins at Puerto Banús, where you’ll meet your skipper and board your chosen yacht. After a short cruise to a calm anchorage (10–20 minutes), your yoga instructor (if booked) will guide you through a 45–60 minute session. The deck is spacious and stable, with mats provided. After yoga, enjoy a light breakfast of snacks and cava or a healthy smoothie from the onboard selection. You can also take a dip in the sea or simply relax as the sun climbs higher. The total charter time is flexible — choose 2, 4, or 8 hours depending on your schedule.

Best Anchorages for Sunrise Yoga Near Marbella

Our skippers know the quietest coves for early-morning yoga. Favourite spots include Cala del Faro (a rocky cove east of Marbella), Cala Cortés (sheltered and shallow), and the waters off the Golden Mile (close to Puerto Banús). All offer calm seas, minimal swell, and stunning views of La Concha. The skipper will choose the best location based on wind and tide conditions on the day.

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does a sunrise yoga yacht charter start in Marbella?

Sunrise charters typically depart between 07:00 and 08:00, depending on the season. We recommend arriving at Puerto Banús 15 minutes early. The exact time is confirmed upon booking, as sunrise varies from around 07:15 in winter to 06:45 in summer.

Do I need to bring my own yoga mat?

No, we can arrange yoga mats and an instructor for your session at an additional cost. Simply let us know when you book via WhatsApp. Mats are stored safely on board, and the instructor will lead a gentle flow suitable for all levels.

Which yacht is best for a sunrise yoga session?

The Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) or Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax) are ideal for small groups, starting at €749 for 2 hours. For larger parties, the Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) offers ample deck space. All depart from Puerto Banús.

What is included in the charter price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, insurance, Spanish IVA (21%), water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, and light snacks. Yoga mats and instructor are not included but can be arranged on request.

Can we anchor for a swim after yoga?

Absolutely. After your sunrise session, the skipper can take you to a calm cove like Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés for a refreshing dip. The water is usually calm and warm in the early morning, especially from May to October.

How do I book a sunrise yoga yacht charter in Marbella?

Simply contact us via WhatsApp with your preferred date, group size, and any requests (yoga instructor, duration). We’ll confirm availability and send a booking link. Payment is secure, and you’ll receive full details before departure.

Is the yoga suitable for beginners?

Yes, the instructor tailors the session to all levels, from complete beginners to experienced practitioners. The gentle movement on the water adds a unique element of balance and mindfulness. Just let us know your experience level when booking.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Your safety is our priority. If conditions are unsafe (e.g., high winds or rough seas), we’ll reschedule your charter or offer a full refund. The skipper monitors forecasts closely and will advise you 24 hours before departure.
