Why Choose a DJ Boat Party in Marbella?

Marbella's coastline offers calm waters, reliable sunshine (averaging 320 days a year), and some of the best sunset spots in Europe. A DJ boat party gives you total control over the vibe: anchor at a secluded cove, cruise past the Golden Mile mansions, or drop anchor off Cabopino beach. The sound carries across the water, and the party becomes part of the scenery.

Unlike a club, there's no queue, no dress code, and no last call. You decide when to turn up the volume, when to jump in for a swim, and when to head back to port.

Sound Systems & DJ Setup

Our boats come with standard marine-grade audio systems. For a DJ boat party, you have three options:

  • Bring your own Bluetooth speaker — JBL PartyBox 310 or similar works well on deck. Battery life 8+ hours.
  • Bring your own DJ equipment — small controller + laptop or USB. We provide power via 230V inverter on larger boats.
  • Hire a local DJ — we can recommend DJs who specialise in boat parties. Rates from €250 for 3 hours. Contact us via WhatsApp.

For serious sound, the Mangusta 80's flybridge offers the best platform for speakers. The Azimut 58 Flybridge has a large aft deck that works well for a DJ booth.

Sunset Timing & Routes

The best time for a DJ boat party in Marbella is sunset — typically 18:00 to 21:00 (May–September). The sun sets behind La Concha mountain, painting the sky orange and pink. Popular routes:

  • Puerto Banús → Cabopino (20 min) — anchor off the dunes, shallow water for swimming.
  • Puerto Banús → Cala del Faro (30 min) — secluded cove, great for photos.
  • Puerto Banús → Estepona (45 min) — longer cruise, see the coast from the sea.
  • Puerto Banús → Sotogrande (1 hour) — luxury marina, return at dusk.

Your skipper will choose the best route based on wind and sea conditions. All routes include a swimming stop if you want to cool off between sets.

Frequently Asked Questions

What boats are available for a DJ boat party in Marbella?

We offer all 12-passenger boats from our fleet: Mangusta 80 (24m), Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m), Canados 86 (26m), Ferretti 94 (29m), and K80 (24m). Each can accommodate up to 12 guests. Smaller groups (up to 10 pax) can use the Pershing 46 or Fairline Targa. All depart from Puerto Banús.

Do you provide the DJ equipment or do I bring my own?

You can bring your own DJ and equipment, or we can arrange a local DJ for you. Our boats have standard marine-grade audio systems; for a professional DJ setup we recommend bringing a portable battery-powered speaker (e.g., JBL PartyBox) or we can arrange a PA system on request. Please contact us via WhatsApp to discuss specific sound requirements.

What is included in the DJ boat party price?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, insurance, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, and light snacks. For Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39) prices start at €749 for 2 hours and go up to €2,299 for 8 hours. Tier B (Mangusta 80) is €4,719 for a minimum 4 hours and includes a free jet ski. All prices include Spanish IVA (21%).

Can we play our own music through the boat's speakers?

Yes, all our boats have Bluetooth or AUX connectivity so you can stream from your phone or laptop. For a proper party atmosphere, we recommend bringing a dedicated Bluetooth speaker or arranging a portable DJ system. The Mangusta 80 and Ferretti 94 have particularly good marine audio systems, but for serious bass you'll want external speakers.

What is the best time for a DJ boat party in Marbella?

Sunset (around 18:00–21:00 from May to September) is the most popular slot. The light is golden, the sea is calm, and you get the famous Marbella sunset behind La Concha mountain. Daytime parties (12:00–16:00) are also popular for swimming stops. Night charters are possible but require advance booking.

How many people can attend a DJ boat party?

Maximum 12 guests on any of our boats. This is a strict safety limit enforced by Spanish maritime regulations. For larger groups, you can book multiple boats or consider a shore party. The Mangusta 80, Azimut 58 Flybridge, Canados 86, Ferretti 94, and K80 all accommodate 12 pax.

Where do we depart from and where can we go?

All boats depart from Puerto Banús, Marbella. Popular destinations include Cabopino (20 min), Cala del Faro (30 min), Estepona (45 min), and Sotogrande (1 hour). Your skipper will recommend the best route based on weather and your preferences. You can also anchor for swimming at Cala Cortés or near the Golden Mile.

How do I book a DJ boat party in Marbella?

Contact us via WhatsApp on +34 684 44 94 70. Provide your preferred date, number of guests, and boat preference. We'll confirm availability and send a booking link. Payment is required in advance to secure the slot. Same-day bookings are possible subject to availability.
