For those seeking the pinnacle of luxury on the Costa del Sol, a superyacht charter Marbella offers unmatched space, style, and sea views. Our fleet includes three exceptional vessels: the Mangusta 80 (24m), the Ferretti 94 (29m), and the Canados 86 (26m), all departing from Puerto Banús. Why Choose a Superyacht Charter in Marbella?

Marbella’s coastline is tailor-made for superyacht cruising. From the glitz of Puerto Banús to the secluded coves of Cala Cortés and the chic marina of Sotogrande, you’ll discover a different perspective of the Mediterranean. A superyacht gives you the space to entertain, sunbathe on expansive decks, and dine alfresco. With 12 guests maximum, you’ll have privacy and comfort. All our charters are all-inclusive: no surprise bills for fuel or drinks.

Mangusta 80: Speed, Style and a Free Jet Ski

The Mangusta 80 is a 24-metre open sport yacht known for its sleek lines and impressive speed. With capacity for 12 guests, it’s ideal for a lively day out. The charter price of €4,719 for a minimum 4 hours includes a jet ski — perfect for exploring hidden coves. Departing from Puerto Banús, you can reach Estepona in about 30 minutes or head to Cala del Faro for a swim stop. We have two colour variants: white and grey, both identical in layout.

Ferretti 94: The Ultimate Flybridge Experience

At 29 metres, the Ferretti 94 is the largest superyacht in our fleet. It offers a spacious flybridge with panoramic views, a large sundeck, and a luxurious interior. Ideal for corporate events or milestone celebrations, this yacht can accommodate 12 guests in supreme comfort. Pricing is customised to your itinerary — contact us via WhatsApp for a quote. Typical routes include a cruise along the Golden Mile to Sotogrande, with lunch at a beach club.

Canados 86: Classic Elegance with Modern Amenities

The Canados 86 combines Italian design with robust sea-keeping. At 26 metres, it features a spacious main deck, a flybridge with seating, and a swim platform for easy water access. Like the Ferretti 94, pricing is on request. This yacht is a favourite for sunset charters from Puerto Banús to Cala Cortés, where you can anchor for a swim as the sun sets behind La Concha mountain.

Popular Destinations from Puerto Banús

Your skipper will tailor the route to your preferences. Common options include:

  • Sotogrande — exclusive marina with beach clubs, 45 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a superyacht charter in Marbella?

All our superyacht charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment and Spanish IVA (21%). There are no hidden extras.

How much does it cost to charter a Mangusta 80 in Marbella?

The Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) starts at €4,719 for a minimum of 4 hours, which includes a free jet ski. Longer durations are available on request.

Can I charter a Ferretti 94 or Canados 86 for a day trip?

Yes, both the Ferretti 94 (29m) and Canados 86 (26m) are available for half-day or full-day charters. Pricing is on request via WhatsApp, as each trip is tailored.

Where can I go on a superyacht charter from Puerto Banús?

Popular destinations include Cala del Faro, Cala Cortés, Estepona, Sotogrande, and along the Golden Mile. Your skipper will suggest the best route based on weather and your preferences.

How many guests can a superyacht in Marbella accommodate?

Our superyachts (Mangusta 80, Ferretti 94, Canados 86) each accommodate up to 12 guests. Smaller vessels are available for groups of 5–11.

Do I need a boating license to charter a superyacht in Marbella?

No. A licensed skipper is included with every charter, so you can relax and enjoy the experience without any paperwork or experience.

Is the price for a superyacht charter per person or per boat?

Prices are per boat, not per person. You can split the cost among your group. For example, the Mangusta 80 at €4,719 for 4 hours works out at under €400 per person for 12 guests.

Can I book a superyacht for a special event like a birthday or proposal?

Absolutely. We cater for celebrations, corporate events, and proposals. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss custom catering, decorations, or additional services.
