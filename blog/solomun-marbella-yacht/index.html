Why Choose a Solomun Marbella Yacht Charter?

Solomun's pre-set parties at Puerto Banús attract thousands of electronic music fans every August. A private yacht charter lets you escape the crowds, anchor at exclusive coves like Cala del Faro or Cala Cortés, and enjoy the sunset with your own music. Our BYO speaker policy means you can connect your Bluetooth speaker and play your favourite sets — from deep house to techno — while sipping cava. The sea breeze and 24°C water make it the ultimate pre-party warm-up.

BYO Speaker Set-Up: How It Works

Our boats come with a standard sound system, but for the Solomun pre-set, we encourage you to bring your own Bluetooth speaker. Simply connect it to your phone or laptop, and you're the DJ. We recommend a portable speaker with at least 8 hours of battery life and IPX7 waterproofing — the JBL Boombox 3 or UE Hyperboom are popular choices. For larger groups, some boats have auxiliary inputs for a professional set-up. Just let us know in advance if you need power outlets for charging.

Best Anchor Spots Near Puerto Banús

During your charter, explore these prime spots for swimming and partying:

  • Cala del Faro – A secluded cove 2 NM west of Puerto Banús, with crystal-clear water and a rocky shoreline. Perfect for anchoring and playing your speaker at medium volume.
  • Cala Cortés – 3 NM east, near Marbella Marina. A sandy beach with calm waters, ideal for a sunset dip.
  • Cabopino – 5 NM east, with a protected bay and the famous sand dunes. Less crowded than Marbella beaches.
  • Estepona – 10 NM west, offering a longer cruise with views of La Concha mountain. Anchor near the old town for a quick tapas break.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring my own speaker on the yacht for Solomun?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own Bluetooth speaker on any of our boats for the Solomun pre-set party. Our charters include a standard sound system on most vessels, but for a BYO set-up, you can connect your own speaker to play your favourite tracks. Just ensure it's portable and doesn't exceed the boat's power limits.

What boats are available for the Solomun pre-set party?

We offer 17 boats from Puerto Banús, including the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax, €749/2h), Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax), Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax, from €4,719/4h with free jet ski), and the Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax). Smaller options like the Speedboat (7m, 6 pax) are also available. All are ideal for a pre-set cruise.

How do I book a Solomun Marbella yacht charter?

Booking is straightforward. Visit our homepage at Boat Rental Marbella, browse the fleet, and contact us via WhatsApp for availability. Tier A boats can be booked directly online; for Tier B and other boats, we provide instant pricing on request. We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for Solomun's pre-set dates in August.

What is included in the charter for the Solomun party?

Every charter includes a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, safety equipment, and Spanish IVA (21% VAT). You can add extra catering or a DJ set-up on request. The BYO speaker is not provided but is allowed at no extra cost.

Where do we depart from for the Solomun pre-set?

All our boats depart from Puerto Banús, the iconic marina in Marbella. This is the central hub for Solomun's pre-set parties, with easy access to the best anchor spots like Cala del Faro and Cala Cortés. The marina is 10 minutes from Marbella's Golden Mile and 30 minutes from Estepona.

What is the best time to charter for the Solomun pre-set?

Solomun's pre-set parties typically run from late afternoon to sunset, so a 4-hour charter from 16:00 to 20:00 works perfectly. This allows you to cruise along the coast, anchor for a swim, and return to Puerto Banús in time for the main event. August sea temperatures average 24°C, ideal for a dip.

Can we bring our own food and drinks on the yacht?

Yes, you are welcome to bring your own food and additional drinks on any charter. Our standard package already includes water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, and light snacks. For larger groups, we can arrange catering from local Marbella restaurants upon request.

How many guests can we bring on the Solomun yacht?

Guest capacity varies by boat: the Astondoa 40 and Azimut 39 hold 9–11 guests, while the Mangusta 80, Azimut 58 Flybridge, and Ferretti 94 each seat up to 12 guests. Smaller boats like the Speedboat (6 pax) or Dubhe (5 pax) are better for intimate groups. Always check the specific boat's capacity when booking.
