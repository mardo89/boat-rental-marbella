Marbella Fashion Week transforms Puerto Banús into a hub of style, glamour and exclusive events. From Press Day previews to runway shows and late-night after-parties, the city buzzes with energy. A Marbella fashion week yacht charter offers a unique way to experience it all — with privacy, luxury and a front-row seat on the water.

What is Marbella Fashion Week?

Marbella Fashion Week is a biannual event showcasing Spanish and international designers. It typically runs for 3–5 days in late spring and early autumn. The programme includes Press Day (for media and influencers), runway shows at venues along the Golden Mile and Puerto Banús, and exclusive after-parties at beach clubs, private villas and superyachts.

Press Day on a Yacht

Press Day is the quietest and most strategic part of the week. Brands present their collections to editors, bloggers and stylists. Instead of a crowded hotel suite, charter a yacht for a relaxed, photogenic setting. The Boat Rental Marbella fleet includes the Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) and Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax) — perfect for intimate gatherings with drinks, snacks and sea views. Both depart from Puerto Banús, minutes from the main venues.

Runway Shows from the Water

Some runway shows take place on the Puerto Banús promenade or at beach clubs like Ocean Club. A yacht anchored nearby gives you an unobstructed view of the catwalk without the crowds. The Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) offers a raised flybridge deck for optimal sightlines. You can watch the show, then cruise along the coast to Cala del Faro for a swim.

After-Parties on Superyachts

After the runway, the party moves to the marina. The biggest after-parties happen on superyachts. Our Marbella fashion week yacht options for parties include:

  • Mangusta 80 (24m, 12 pax) — €4,719 minimum 4 hours, includes a jet ski. Spacious sundeck, bar, and sound system.
  • Ferretti 94 (29m, 12 pax) — pricing on request. Huge flybridge, salon and aft deck for dancing.
  • Canados 86 (26m, 12 pax) — pricing on request. Classic lines with generous outdoor spaces.

All charters include a licensed skipper, fuel, water, soft drinks, beer, white wine, cava, light snacks, insurance, and Spanish VAT (21%).

How to Plan Your Fashion Week Yacht Charter

Here’s what you need to know to book a Marbella fashion week yacht:

  • Book early — yachts fill up fast during the event. Aim for 2–3 weeks ahead.
  • Choose your vessel — for small groups (up to 11 pax), the Azimut 39 or Astondoa 40 work well. For parties, the Mangusta 80 or Ferretti 94.
  • Customise — we can arrange catering, DJs, or a specific route. Contact us via WhatsApp for details.
  • Combine with a cruise — after the show, cruise to Cala Cortés or Estepona for a sunset swim.

Why Choose a Yacht for Fashion Week?

A yacht gives you privacy, flexibility and a stunning backdrop. You control the guest list, the music, and the schedule. Whether you’re hosting a Press Day preview or an after-party, the water adds a touch of exclusivity that no venue can match. And with Puerto Banús at your doorstep, you’re never far from the action.

Frequently asked questions

When is Marbella Fashion Week in 2025?

Marbella Fashion Week usually takes place in May/June and again in September/October. Exact dates are announced a few months ahead by the organisers. We recommend planning your yacht charter early, as demand spikes during the event.

Can I charter a yacht to watch the runway shows from the water?

Yes. Some runway venues are on the Puerto Banús waterfront, and a yacht anchored nearby gives you a front-row view without the crowd. Our boats like the Azimut 58 Flybridge (17.6m, 12 pax) offer an elevated deck for clear sightlines.

Which boat is best for a fashion week after-party?

For a party with up to 12 guests, the Mangusta 80 (24m) or Ferretti 94 (29m) are top choices. Both have spacious sundecks, a bar area, and a sound system. The Mangusta 80 also includes a jet ski for added fun.

Can I host a private Press Day event on a yacht?

Absolutely. A yacht charter is ideal for intimate brand presentations or media previews. The Astondoa 40 (12.5m, 9 pax) or Azimut 39 (12.5m, 11 pax) work well for smaller groups. All charters include drinks and snacks.

Are fashion week yacht charters expensive?

Prices start at €749 for 2 hours on our Tier A boats (Astondoa 40, Azimut 39), which include skipper, drinks and VAT. Larger vessels like the Mangusta 80 start at €4,719 for 4 hours. You can request a quote for other boats via WhatsApp.

How far in advance should I book a yacht for Marbella Fashion Week?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks ahead, especially for the larger yachts. During the event, availability shrinks fast. Contact us via WhatsApp to check dates and secure your preferred vessel.

Can I combine a fashion week charter with a cruise along the coast?

Yes. Most charters can include a scenic cruise to Cala del Faro, Cala Cortés, or even Estepona. You can watch the show or party, then unwind with a sunset sail. Just let us know your preferred route when booking.

Is it possible to have catering or a DJ on board for an after-party?

We can arrange catering upgrades and a DJ setup on request. Larger yachts like the Ferretti 94 have ample deck space for a dance floor. Speak to our team via WhatsApp to customise your event.
